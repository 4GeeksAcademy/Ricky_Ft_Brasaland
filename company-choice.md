## My AI Agent Idea

### Company Context
I am working with a company called Brasaland, a grilled food restaurant chain with 14 locations across Colombia and the United States.

### Business Need
Brasaland needs to relaunch its corporate website to present the brand as a serious and established restaurant chain in both countries.

### Website Goals
The new website should include:
- A landing page that explains Brasaland's value proposition.
- A section that shows locations in Colombia and the United States.
- A section that presents the new digital loyalty program, Brasa Points.
- A registration page with a form where users can sign up for the loyalty program.

### AI Agent Role
The AI agent will create a fully functional, interactive website for customers and employees.

### Data Source
The AI agent will use company data related to customer and employee interactions across Brasaland locations in Miami and Colombia.

### Success Metrics
The project will be considered successful if it achieves the following within the first 90 days after launch:
- Increase loyalty program registrations by at least 25%.
- Reach a landing-page-to-signup conversion rate of at least 8%.
- Reduce loyalty form abandonment rate to below 30%.
- Achieve an average session duration of at least 2 minutes on the landing page.
- Ensure at least 99.5% website uptime and a mobile page load time under 3 seconds.
